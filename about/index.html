Hello, world.

I'm a entrepreneur, consultant, and writer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Currently serving as chief product officer and co-founder of Roscoe Labs, a news technology startup. Our mantra is: "Open media. Open communities."​

Previously, I was an award-winning markets reporter at the Wall Street Journal. I wrote extensively for both the Web and print editions, including daily coverage of the historic stock-market crash in late 2008. In the months following that meltdown, I also provided on-air market analysis for more than 100 broadcast outlets, including CNN, the BBC, CNBC, San Francisco’s KRON and New York’s WCBS.

In general, I'm intrigued by the evolution of media, especially the potential of nimble digital orgs to assume the former role of traditional shops now in decline. I'm also passionate about the open Web and spreading its benefits to more people around the world.
