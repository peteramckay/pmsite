Writing Samples

By Peter McKay

Round 1: The FBI vs. Apple. Round 2: The FBI vs. Journalists?

Commentary for the Poynter Institute's website advocating that all news sites implement encryption to protect user privacy and free-speech rights. Published 3/7/16.

Product managers as editors

Personal blog post about some surprising areas of overlap between my experience as an entrepreneur/product manager and that of working in a newsroom. Published 5/24/13.

Don't do it, Mr. Mayor!

Personal blog post advocating that Mike Bloomberg buy RIM instead of the Financial Times, a rumored acquisition target of his at the time. Published 12/11/12.

'My Confession': Fund Manager Falls Publicly

Feature on a fraudulent money manager who sent his clients an unusual video message admitting his crimes. Aside from being a colorful yarn to tell, this was also one of the first stories on WSJ.com to integrate video gathered by staff rather than our then-broadcast partner CNBC. Published 9/16/04.

While Marc Rich Was Fugitive, Firm Dealt With Pariah Nations

Co-authored investigation of Marc Rich, a longtime fugitive commodity trader who controversally received a pardon from President Clinton on his last night in office. Published 2/23/01.
